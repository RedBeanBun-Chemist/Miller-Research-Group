IConIC Prosperity Partnership

The Miller Group is a co-investigator within the Innovative Continuous Manufacturing for Industrial Chemicals (IConIC) programme — a £17.8M EPSRC Prosperity Partnership led by BASF and Imperial College London. The partnership brings together a consortium of organisations from across the chemicals value chain to transform the manufacture of high-value chemicals through continuous flow processes.
