Repurposing a Greenhouse Gas: Defluoroalkylation of HCF₃ Published in Angew. Chem.

Our collaborative work with the Crimmin group converts trifluoromethane — a potent greenhouse gas with 14,600× the warming potential of CO₂ — into valuable fluoroalkene building blocks via continuous flow methods.

Angew. Chem. Int. Ed. 2026

Open-Source M-Arc Photoreactor Published in React. Chem. Eng.

The modular, 3D-printed M-Arc reactor demonstrated 3.25-fold improved photon delivery compared to commercial systems. All designs are freely available to the community.
